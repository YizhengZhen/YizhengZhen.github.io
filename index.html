About me

My research focuses on quantum information theory and its application in experiments. Specifically, I am interested in

  • Bell nonlocality and its application in quantum information processing;
  • Energy and information cost in (quantum) information processing;
  • Dicke model and its realization in microwave superconducting circuit environment.
My work involves a combination of analytical and numerical methods to explore how to understand quantum system/dynamics from the information perspective and how such understanding can be demonstrated/applied in experiments.
