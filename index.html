Welcome to my homepage!

I am currently a Research Associate at University of Science and Technology of China.

My research interest lies in the fields of quantum foundation, quantum information, and information thermodynamics.

I am particularly interested in the following topics:

  • Bell nonlocality and its application in quantum information processing;
  • Energy and information cost in (quantum) information processing;
  • Dicke model and its realization in microwave superconducting circuit environment.

My research works can be found in arXiv, ORCID, and Google Scholar.
